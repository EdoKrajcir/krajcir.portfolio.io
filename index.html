Who I am?

My name is Eduard Krajčír, and I am 22 at this point of my life. I live in Ivanka pri Dunaji, which is located on the outskirts of Slovak capital, Bratislava. After getting into the IT faculty of Slovak Technical University (FIIT STU) based in Bratislava back in 2017, I`ve started web developing. As a very creative person, I `ve been always into digital art, visualisations and animations from early age. Web designing and developing fulfills all of my visual needs.

Since I was 15, I`ve been creating graphics for skateboard and fingerboard decks and working as a web-shop administrator. I acquired ability to communicate with customers at this point. When I was 18, I immersed myself fully into HTML, CSS and PHP, short after that I added a pinch of SQL, Javascript and software modeling and "VOILA"...Freshly baked web designer and developer was baked.

What I do?

It is very simple, I focus on doing nice, original, intuitive, effective and responsive websides and web applications. Nowadays, there is a proverb: Who is not on the web, as if he didnt existed...

Remember! Responsivity is a key! Based on studies, in 2020, 55.61% web visits were made via mobile devices. Therefore I am mainly using mobile-first design, where web is designed for mobile devices first, and then adapted for larger devices such as desktops. This technique provides nice to look at, responsive web. No compromises need to be made then.
